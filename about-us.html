The Origin of Bluebird Co.

The story behind our group stems from our early childhoods. Not directly knowing each other yet, divided between skiing and snowboarding, we all grew up skiing and snowboarding at Toggenburg Ski Mountain in Syracuse, New York.

Skiers

Introduced to each other at the ages of: five (Matt and David), three (Ryan), and one (Andrew); the four of us grew up as neighbors on the West Side of Caznovia, New York. Our early passions to stray from the norm ignited our love to romp. We quickly began riding bikes, inline skates, skateboards, scootering, waterskiing, you name it in an attempt to sastify our hunger. Subsequently, our love for the outdoors quickly led to our discovery of skiing.

Pictured from left to right: Ryan Mistur, David Peterson, Andrew Peterson, Matt Mistur (circa. 2004)

Snowboarders

Recurited by Joe, Mac and Thomas were united to become the boarders we know today. Growing up snowboarding Syracuse Urban with the help of Joe, the crew began making a name for themselves when videos emerged of the three greasing dicey hand rails around the city.

Presently

The seven of us currently comprise what is know today as Bluebird Co., stemming from the term "Bluebird Day" in the world of snow sports. We are continuing to fuel our passions to seek new challenges. To bodly go where no one has gone before.
